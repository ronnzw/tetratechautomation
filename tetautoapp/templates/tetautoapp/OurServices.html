Our Services

Industrial Automation System

We take your problem definition, study your plant operation or desired operation and evaluate the requirements. We offer vast services in this area from designing, layout drawing, programming/reprogramming of instrumentation components and implementation.

  • PLC Programming
  • Third Party Systems Integration e.g VFD, Scanners
  • SCADA Development
  • Typical electrical schematics
Manufacturing Execution Systems

We take automation to the next level by interfacing the control system with the business systems to achieve production resource planning and scheduling, production performance management and integration with Enterprise Resource Planning systems.

  • Production management
  • Inventory operations management
  • Plant performance management
  • Quality management by interfacing to Laboratory Information Management Systems
Motor Control and Protection Systems

We offer comprehensive design of Motor Control Centres from the traditional types to the modern networked smart solutions. We design and manufacture MCCs as standalone solution and as an integral part of industrial automation turnkey solutions.

  • MV Soft Starters
  • MV Variable Frequency Drives
  • Direct online Circuit Breaker switched systems
  • Direct online contactor switched drive systems
  • Liquid Resistance Starters, for current limiting, torque or stability control
